Mapping the Riverfront

Visualizing downtown Owensboro in Daviess County, KY

Project Overview

The Riverfront in downtown Owensboro, KY was the center for industry and commerce in Daviess County throughout much of the early 20th century. Over the last 10-15 years, the area has been under redevelopment and revitilization in an effort to preserve the historic sections of downtown and transform the riverfront into an area for entertainment and leisure.

The goal of this project was to develop a LIDAR point cloud and high-resolution imagery for the modern Owensboro Riverfront. Using these visualizations, I was then able to create a Cesium slideshow and present the data in an interactive format that can provide some insight into this unique area of my hometown.

Visualizations created from lidar data provided by KyFromAbove in ArcGIS Pro and Blender, April, 2023.

Page and visualizations created by Lucas Ruckdeschel for GEO 409, Department of Geography, University of Kentucky. Spring 2023.
